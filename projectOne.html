THE DESKTOP COMPUTER BASED TEST APP FOR SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL STUDENTS

My role: UI/UX designer | Duration: 1 month


Overview

This desktop app design was done for DevlanLabs. DevlanLabs is a tech company in Osun state Nigeria that is passionate about improving the quality of life of people through innovations that are capable of simplifying users' day-to-day activities.

The App was designed to familiarize senior secondary school students in Osun State with the use of the computer, computer-based tests and prepare them for the joint admission matriculation board examination which will help them to gain admission into the higher institution.




The Problem

DevlanLabs conducted a quantitative study in Osun State and discovered that most secondary school students in the state don't get to familiarize themselves with computer-based tests till they write their first joint admission matriculation board examination (JAMB). Aside the fact that some do not even know how to use a computer, others spend too much time understanding the new exam interface they see on the JAMB examination day. As a result of this, they always find it difficult to effectively utilize their JAMB exam time and answer more questions correctly.

Another problem discovered was the huge amount of money spent by these schools on question papers every year. Usually, this cost is passed down to the poor students which further increases their school fees. After spending much money on exams, some schools do have issues with missing exam papers while others are left with a huge number of scripts to mark manually. All these problems are what the desktop CBT app by DevlanLabs was designed to solve.




The User and Audience

The CBT app was designed for Osun state public and private senior secondary school students and their teachers. Most times, students from private schools do have more access to computers than their counterparts from public schools.

This is because they are from rich families where there is easy access to laptops. However, most senior secondary school students from the state are smart and intelligent.


My roles and responsibilities

The team for this project was small. I was the only UI/UX designer who worked on the project from the beginning to the end. I was responsible for the wireframing, prototyping, and user testing process of the design.

Challenges faced

As the only designer who worked on the project, I had to ensure that I delivered great work within a short period without altering the quality which required much dedication and hard work.




The Process

I created user personas for each of the categories of the targeted users ( senior secondary school student and their teachers). Based on the interview I had with DevlanLabs, I drafted the user story and created the wireframe for the desktop app in Figma, where I allocated different parts of the screens to functional elements.

A design system was created to speed up the design process, boost efficiency and ensure consistency and harmony within the design. Using the design system and the wireframes, a high-fidelity design was done where the colors and text styles were implemented.

User testing

The high fidelity prototype was tested with two groups of users; the students and the teachers. Most of the students navigated the app with ease. However, some of the teachers had problems with certain aspects of the admin dashboard. This was adjusted to ensure a better user experience on the desktop app.



Lessons learned

This design project taught me how to deliver great design within a short period without compromising quality.
